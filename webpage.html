How To Make Soup

Soup is a great snack/meal for cold winter months. But it can be eaten year round. Eating soup is healthy for you-as long as the soup is low in sodium.

Many canned soup companies have an enormous amount of sodium and cholesterol in their soups.

Check out recipes

By making your own soup, you can control the amountof sodium and other unhealthy additives that are found in brands like, 'Campbell's' and 'Progresso'.
